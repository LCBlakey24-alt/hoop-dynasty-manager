# Hoop Dynasty Manager Data Notes

## Current Team Data

The app currently includes all 12 planned British Super Basketball League teams.

Each team currently has a seeded starting five only:

- 1 point guard
- 1 shooting guard
- 1 small forward
- 1 power forward
- 1 centre

This gives the project enough data to build the first dashboard, team selection, basic roster screen, and early match simulation.

## Next Data Step

Expand each team from 5 players to 10 players.

Recommended roster shape per team:

- 5 starters
- 3 bench rotation players
- 1 veteran/depth player
- 1 young prospect
